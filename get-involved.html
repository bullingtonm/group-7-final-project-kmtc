Kennesaw Mountain Needs YOU!

The Kennesaw Mountain Trail Club (KMTC) is a group of volunteers dedicated to preserving the beauty & history of Kennesaw Mountain National Battlefield Park. Since our beginning in 2002, the Trail Club has contributed over 211,181 volunteer hours donated by the public to enhance the trail system within the park!

Kennesaw Mountain again topped the charts in 2020 as the most-visited Civil War battlefield park in the country! In 2014, Kennesaw Mountain NB Park had 2,360,256 visitors and in 2020 we had 2,356,401visitors far surpassing:

  • Gettysburg 536,533
  • Chickamauga/Chattanooga 678,414
  • Fredericksburg/Spotsylvania 838,835
  • Vicksburg 176,236
  • Manassas 558,036
